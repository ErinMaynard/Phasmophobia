Ghost description and abilities

The demon will spawn in more often than other ghosts.

Testing for Demon

In order to test for a demon you can use smudge sticks. Light the smudge sticks on the ghost's spawn point and immediately start a timer. Begin to anger the ghost (saying its name, lowering sanity, etc) and try to get it to spawn in. The demon is unable to hunt for 60 seconds after being smudged, unlike the normal 90 seconds. If a hunt is initiated before the timer hits 180 seconds, it is a demon. The demon is also the only ghost which will not lower sanity after asking a question on the ouija board.
