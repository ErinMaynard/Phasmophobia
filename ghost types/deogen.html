Ghost description and abilities

The deogen will slow down more the closer it gets to a player. You will not be able to hide from the deogen, it will find you.

Testing for Deogen

Although there is no specific evidence based test for a deogen, it will be obvious during the hunt. If you hear incredibly fast foot steps that slow down as they grow closer, you have a deogen. This is probably the easiest ghost to loop given that the player will be faster than it when it is right next to them.

Additional Info

Speed: 3.0 at furthest distance, 0.4 at closest
Blink: Normal
Starting Hunt Sanity: 40%
