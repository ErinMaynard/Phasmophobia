Ghost description and abilities

The Raiju can interact with electronics from 15 meters away vs the normal 10. Also, when there is at least one piece of equipment active the Raiju will speed up.

Testing for Raiju

In order to test for a Raiju make sure to listen to the ghost while it roams during a hunt. If it is fast moving around electrical equipment and interacts with electrical equipment often, it may be a Raiju.
